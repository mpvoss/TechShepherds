Coders


Tired of "entry-level" jobs requiring 2 years of experience...when you can't get an entry level job to get experience in the first place? Yeah, we were too. If you're a sharp coder that wants resume-building projects more important than the solitaire game you made in CompSci 101 or Conways Game of Life, Tech Shepherds is for you.

Non-profits and small business with real technology needs come to us looking for talent and that's where you come in. These are projects with real quantifiable impact where you can make a difference and seriously dress up your resume.

If you need experience to prepare yourself for a career in software, we have plenty of work for you. Drop an application in the form below and we'll be in touch. There is a very simple assessment process to make sure you know your stuff and then you'll be right to work on real assignments helping people that need your expertise.

Don't stress about the application. We hate "List your top 3 strength and weakness" interviews as much as anyone and we just want to see your code. Seriously. If you have any interest at all, apply! Even if we don't have any projects on hand that match your skillset right now we can give you some feedback and we'll keep you in mind if something comes up.
