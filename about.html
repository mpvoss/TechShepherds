Connecting real purpose with real talent

Technology does an incredible job of connecting people and ideas around the globe. Websites can spread messages and videos, apps can engage users with the mission of a group and databases can organize the data that really matters. A talented programmer can utilize these technoligies to meet almost any requirement.

Not everyone is a code monkey, however, and hiring one can cost you a pretty penny. That's not ideal, especially for nonprofits and groups that are doing important work without massive funding to get it done.

That's where Tech Shepherds comes in.

We connect these groups that have important needs with talented programmers that are still in school or want side projects with good causes. They can do great work and are more cost-effective than their more senior counterparts.
