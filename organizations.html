Organizations

  • Need to update your Wordpress website?
  • Want a mobile app for your organization?
  • Could really use a Ruby on Rails or Django website for your clients?
If you replied yes to any of these than you're probably a great match for Tech Shepherds. These are tasks that even a younger coder can complete very effectively, and often quickly. Focus on running your non-profit or small business and let the programmers do what they do best.

All Tech Shepherds coders are interviewed and vetted by senior Tech Shepherd members. You won't be getting a discount coder that doesn't know what they are doing. These are smart technology workers that are looking for experience and a good cause to utilize their skills.

All Tech Shepherd projects are code reviewed before they go out the door. At least two of our coders will approve your project before it's handed over to you and you can count on it being quality.
